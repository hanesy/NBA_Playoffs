Playoffs Predictions


Introduction


For our final project, we decided to predict the teams that would have made it do the playoffs in 2020. We used historical data of games statistics since 1979 to base our prediction.

Every regular season, there are 30 teams divided into two conferences: west and east. Each team plays 82 games per season, being 41 home and 41 away. At the end of the regular season, the eight teams with the most wins in each conference qualify for the playoffs.

We decided to compare the results of 3 different models to do our predictions, so that we could decide which model would be the most accurate, based on our historical data.


NBA History

Why we chose 1979:

Starting in the 1979-80 NBA season the 3-point line was implemented with the intention of increasing the chances for shorter players to earn more points and to expand defense away from the basket.
